What is KanjiMichi?

KanjiMichi is a simple web-based learning platform aimed at helping beginners master Kanji characters in an easy and practical way.

What are the advantages of KanjiMichi?

KanjiMichi is designed to simplify and accelerate the process of learning Kanji for beginners, with the following advantages:

  • Minimalist Interface Design
  • Interactive Learning Interface
  • Step-by-Step Learning Approach
  • Free Access

Why Does KanjiMichi Exist?

I created KanjiMichi to contribute and help users learn Kanji in an easy and practical way. In addition, this project also opens a path for me to grow in the world of web development, providing personal satisfaction.
